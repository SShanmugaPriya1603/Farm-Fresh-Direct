Government Schemes & Insurance

Explore programs designed to protect your crops and support your livelihood.

Crop & Weather Insurance

PMFBY - Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

Provider: Government of India

  • Coverage: Yield loss due to non-preventable risks (drought, flood, pests).
  • Premium: 2% for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi, 5% for commercial crops.
Learn More & Apply

Weather Based Crop Insurance Scheme (WBCIS)

Provider: Various Public/Private Insurers

  • Coverage: Losses due to adverse weather like rainfall deficit, temperature fluctuations.
  • Premium: Varies by crop and location.
Learn More & Apply

Coconut Palm Insurance Scheme (CPIS)

Provider: Coconut Development Board

  • Coverage: Provides insurance for coconut palms against risks like pests, diseases, and natural disasters.
  • Premium: Subsidized premium shared between the Board, State Govt, and farmer.

Income & Development Support

PM-KISAN Scheme

Provider: Government of India

  • Benefit: Income support of ₹6,000 per year in three equal installments.
  • Eligibility: All landholding farmer families.
Check Eligibility & Status

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

Provider: Government of India

  • Objective: To promote organic farming and improve soil health.
  • Benefit: Financial assistance for cluster formation and certification.
